Site Plan: Gentizi Home & Deco

Site Name

Gentizi Home & Deco – This name represents a modern online store offering home furniture and decoration products. It's short, memorable, and aligned with the aesthetics of the brand.

Site Purpose

The website is designed for online shopping. It allows customers to browse, explore, and buy products like kitchens, TV panels, wardrobes, and home decor furniture in a convenient and modern way.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the home decoration products that match my style?
  • What are the available payment and delivery options for Gentizi furniture?

Color Schema

Primary color: Yellow (#FFD700) – Used in headings, buttons, and accents.
Secondary color: Dark gray (#666666) – Used for text and borders.

Typography

Main font: Segoe UI – Used for both headings and body text for a clean, modern look.
